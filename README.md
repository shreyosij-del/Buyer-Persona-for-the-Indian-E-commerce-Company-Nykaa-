# Buyer-Persona-for-the-Indian-E-commerce-Company-Nykaa-
Nykaa: Buyer Persona & Customer Journey Analysis
This repository contains a comprehensive case study on Nykaa, India's leading omnichannel beauty and lifestyle platform. The project explores the brand's rapid growth, the intricacies of its customer journey, and a detailed profile of its target buyer persona.


Project Overview
Founded in 2012 by Falguni Nayar, Nykaa (FSN E-Commerce Ventures Ltd.) has revolutionized the Indian beauty market through a successful marketplace business model. This project analyzes how the brand leverages authenticity, variety, and influencer marketing to dominate the retail space both online and via its 100+ physical stores.



Key Research Areas
1. Reasons for Rapid Growth

Trust in Authenticity: Utilizing an inventory-led model to ensure genuine products.


Variety & Exclusivity: Offering a wide assortment of both international and Indian brands with exclusive launches.


Influencer Marketing: Partnering with a range of creators, from macro-celebrities like Katrina Kaif to micro-influencers.


Omnichannel Expansion: Increasing physical store networks in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities.

2. The Customer Journey
The project maps the five critical stages of the Nykaa consumer experience:


Awareness: Discovery via Instagram/YouTube ads, Nykaa Beauty Book articles, or word-of-mouth.



Consideration: Exploring products on the app, watching Nykaa TV tutorials, and shortlisting items during sales like "Pink Friday".



Decision: Completing purchases using secure digital wallets, Nykaa Prive perks, or COD.



Loyalty: Regular engagement through personalized recommendations and hybrid shopping (online and Luxe/On Trend stores).



Advocacy: Recommending the brand to peers and sharing unboxing videos or reviews on social media.
